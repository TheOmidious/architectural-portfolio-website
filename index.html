About

I’m an architect with a Master’s degree in Architecture, designing buildings that won’t collapse and spaces that wow. Think of me as the superhero of blueprints, wielding a T-square instead of a cape. Currently, I'm learning to code, adding another arrow to my quiver. Picture an architect who can not only build your dream house but also debug your WiFi issues. Yep, that’s the goal. I want to merge architecture and technology to create the ultimate smart buildings. Imagine a house that knows when you’ve run out of coffee and orders it for you. That’s the kind of future I’m designing – functional, fun, and fantastically futuristic.
